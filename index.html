My Developer Journey

In 2014, I had seen my friends take the opportunity to learn to code and totally change their lives for the better. I wanted to see the same thing happen in my own life and enrolled in Dev Bootcamp. There, I learned Ruby and JavaScript and discovered tools to learn any other programming language. I also gained substantial knowledge in pair programming, developed proficiency in test-driven and agile development, and learned best practices in client-side application interface and interactivity. I also gained essential team skills and learned how to create a supportive and health-oriented environment.

As a final project, I developed an app that reads your email and gives you feedback on tone and clarity. I divided my time between working on main features, test coverage, and was the lead with UX/UI. To continue my education, I am enrolled in Code School to learn the fundamentals of design, front-end development, and crafting user experience that are easy on the eyes. I am also honing my knowledge of building lightweight applications with enhanced user interfaces.
